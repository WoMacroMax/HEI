About Community Family Clinic

Founded in 2005, Community Family Clinic has been serving our local community with compassionate, high-quality healthcare for the entire family. Our mission is to provide accessible, comprehensive medical care that addresses the unique needs of each patient.

Our Mission

To improve the health and wellbeing of our community by providing exceptional, patient-centered healthcare services with compassion and respect.

Our Values
  • Excellence in healthcare delivery
  • Compassion and empathy for all patients
  • Integrity and ethical practice
  • Accessible and affordable care
  • Continuous improvement and innovation

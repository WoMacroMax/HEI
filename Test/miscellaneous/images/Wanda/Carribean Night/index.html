About Wanda’s

Since 2012, Wanda’s Restaurant of Bayonne has celebrated Black culinary traditions with a modern twist. Chef Wanda Pierre curates seasonal menus inspired by southern comfort classics, Caribbean staples, and the rich diversity of Hudson County. Join us for date night, brunch, or a catered event that keeps guests talking.

    Experience authentic Southern flavors at 906-908 Broadway, Bayonne, NJ 07002. Ms. Wanda's offers more than just a meal—it's a celebration of tradition, community, and culinary excellence. Step into a warm, blues-era inspired atmosphere where every dish tells a story.

    Menu Highlights:

    • Crispy Fried Seafood with collard greens & cornbread
    • Stuffed Red Snapper with yams & potato salad
    • Shrimp Scampi Pie with garlic knots
    • Buffalo Wings, Eggplant Rollitini & More

    Ms. Wanda’s Boutique Service

    From intimate tastings to milestone celebrations, our boutique team curates end-to-end hospitality with grace and precision. Expect menu design tailored to your guests, white-glove presentation, and a service cadence that never rushes a moment. We handle rentals and styling, coordinate timelines with your photographer or planner, and can accommodate dietary preferences (halal, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free) without compromising flavor.

    • Private dining & chef’s table experiences
    • Corporate luncheons, offsites, and VIP green room catering
    • Custom canapé & dessert bars, mocktail pairings
    • On-site carving & live action stations
    • Full event staffing, linen & tableware coordination

    Political & Community Events

    We proudly host nonpartisan community gatherings and civic programming—creating a warm, respectful space for neighbors to connect. Whether it’s a town hall, candidate meet-and-greet, cultural celebration, or fundraiser, our team manages hospitality so organizers can focus on the conversation.

    • Town halls & issue forums (podium, PA, mic setup)
    • Meet-and-greets, listening sessions, and press briefings
    • Voter-registration & resource drives (in partnership with local orgs)
    • School, church, and youth-program fundraisers
    • Local artist showcases & heritage month celebrations

    Event support options: staging & backdrops, press step-and-repeat, run-of-show planning, security liaison, ADA-conscious seating, and media check-in. To inquire, call +1 (703) 596-2482 or email events@mswandas.example.

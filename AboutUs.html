About Us

Our Mission

Health Equity Impact is a mission-driven nonprofit Site Management Organization (SMO) dedicated to advancing diversity in clinical research. We focus on selecting and supporting research sites that investigate sickle cell anemia, rare diseases, and chronic diseases, ensuring that underrepresented populations are included in the pursuit of equitable healthcare outcomes. We are an SMO dedicated to managing diverse research sites, ensuring successful patient enrollment, and optimizing retention for high-quality clinical trial outcomes.
Our Goals

  • Deliver equitable healthcare
  • Diversify clinical trials
  • Foster health equity for underserved communities
  • Build community partnerships
  • Guide individuals to engage in clinical trials
  • Cultivate community alliances
